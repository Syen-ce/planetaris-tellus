# Welcome to Tellus

Tellus is a planet full of life, mycelia and chlorophyll, infected by dangerous parasites and clouds of pollen corrode materials. Automate very risky new materials and make sure your base dont get destroyed from the inside.Create and plant living machinery and try to keep it alive. You can visit tellus after completing gleba.

Tellus can be played on its own but is recommended to play with the rest of the [Planetaris](https://mods.factorio.com/mod/planetaris-unbounded) mods.

# Inside Tellus

**(This section contains spoilers)**

### Living machinery

Collect magnesium and create your fist living machine, Bioassemblers! Plant them, whatch them grow and produce with them, they consume chlorophyll from roots and with time will die, revive them by feeding nutrients before they decompose.

<img style="width:auto;" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8ec128fa-deb7-4b3e-b610-f16df29bc332" />

### Wasps

Tellus is infested with wasps, insects more dangerous than biters and pentapods. They are fast and when they are big can fly over walls. Take their eggs and and process them to make even more dangerous materials

<img style="width:auto;" alt="tellus-1" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/46d961c5-9ec9-4ae2-bdf7-d4db2d506665" />

### Pollen & air cleaning

Be careful harvesting or placing too much machines if you dont want to produce too much pollen, wasps are atracted to it and will use it to become stronger. The high concentration of pollen on the atmosphere will corrode some builds like pipes, roboports or beacons making them weaker and more slow.

Build the air purifier to clean pollen from the atmoshpere, insert water and it will output infected water, find a way to clean it!

<img style="width:auto;" alt="tellus-corrosion" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/dea3a11f-8990-46d7-b460-23f69ea38a55" />

### Agriculture

Harvest and process tellus plants to start your factory. Chloroplasts to get fruits and chlorophyll to feed your machines. Myceliae to get mushroom caps and water, one of the most necesary materials on tellus. Harvests Mushells to get wood.
